HINTS

  1. Use the survival instinct function, it will make everything easier
  2. Tap the LB-button to trigger the survival instincts
  3. The hunter has the most ammo capacity
  4. Axe Strikes: Stun enemies with the Axe
  5. Shotgun Expert: Additional EXP for finishing the enemies with the Shotgun after dodging a melee attack
  6. Climber's Agility: Increases climbing skills and resistance when falling from heights
  7. Dodge Kill: Execute light-armoured enemies after dodging
  8. The Brawler-category will support you on how to fight the enemies later in the game
